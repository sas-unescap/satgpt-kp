Get Started in 5 Minutes

Follow these steps to begin analyzing satellite data with SatGPT

Create Your Account

Sign up for a free SatGPT Hub account to access all platform features including case studies, data dashboards, and eLearning modules.

Set Up Google Earth Engine

Register for Google Earth Engine access to process satellite imagery at scale. This is required for most analysis workflows.

// Initialize Earth Engine
ee.Initialize()

Explore Sample Datasets

Browse our curated collection of analysis-ready datasets covering agriculture, water resources, disasters, and urban development across Asia-Pacific.

Run Your First Analysis

Use our pre-built templates to perform common analyses like NDVI computation, flood mapping, or land cover classification.

// Compute NDVI
var ndvi = image.normalizedDifference(['B8', 'B4']);

Share Your Results

Export your analysis results, create interactive maps, and share insights with stakeholders through our dashboard tools.

About Us

About SatGPT Hub

Empowering sustainable development through satellite intelligence and AI-driven geospatial solutions

UN ESCAP

Our parent organization

The United Nations Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific (ESCAP) is the regional development arm of the United Nations for the Asia-Pacific region. With 53 Member States and 9 Associate Members, ESCAP is the most comprehensive of the five regional commissions.

SatGPT Hub is an initiative of ESCAP's Space Applications Section, working to democratize access to satellite data and AI-powered geospatial analysis for sustainable development across the region.

Our Mission

To strengthen the capacity of ESCAP member states to leverage satellite technology and artificial intelligence for evidence-based decision making, particularly in support of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.

Key Focus Areas

  • Disaster risk reduction and early warning systems
  • Agricultural monitoring and food security
  • Water resources management
  • Urban development and planning
  • Climate change monitoring and adaptation
  • SDG indicator monitoring
